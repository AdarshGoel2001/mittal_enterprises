- Completely self – contained : no other measuring instruments are needed.
- No calibration required.
- High sensitivity : change of the order of 0.1 nf can easily be detected.
- Integrated circuit electronics.
- Linearity better than 1% f.s.d.

In operation it produces an output current proportional to capacitance.

## Description

The Kit consists of reed relay switch with it’s a.c. supply, integrated circuit current amplifier and 0-100 micrometer housed in a cabinet. In addition, a pair of capacitor plates 0.25m x 0.25m x 4 mm, a Perspex sheet, two capacitors (500 pf each), and set of perspex/ polythene spacers are provided with the kit.
